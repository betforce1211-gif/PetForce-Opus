# Welcome Email Sequence

**Audience:** New beta users after account creation
**Sequence:** 3 emails over 7 days
**Goal:** Drive household setup → first activity log → invite a second member

---

## Email 1: Welcome + Quick Setup

**Trigger:** Immediately after account creation
**Subject:** "Welcome to PetForce — let's set up your household"
**From:** The PetForce Team <hello@petforce.app>

---

Hi {{first_name}},

Welcome to PetForce. You just created your account — now let's make it useful.

**Three things to do in the next 5 minutes:**

**1. Name your household**
This is the shared space where everyone coordinates pet care. Give it a name your family will recognize — "The Johnsons" or "Casa de Chaos" both work.

**2. Add your first pet**
Name, species, breed, and any medical notes worth knowing. Takes about 30 seconds.

**3. Log your first activity**
Fed the dog? Walked the cat? (Yes, some people walk cats.) Tap the quick-log button and record it. Under 3 seconds.

Once you've done those three things, you'll see why PetForce exists: a clean record of who did what, when, for every pet in your household.

Tomorrow, we'll show you how to invite your family so everyone's on the same page.

## Email 2: Invite Your Household

**Trigger:** Day 3 after signup
**Subject:** "Pet care works better with two (or more)"
**Condition:** Send only if user has NOT yet invited a household member

---

Hi {{first_name}},

PetForce works for one person — but it's built for households.

Here's what changes when someone else joins:

- **No more "did you feed the dog?" texts.** Everyone sees the activity log in real time.
- **Schedules stay visible.** If your partner walked Luna this morning, you'll know before you grab the leash.
- **Nothing gets missed.** Medication reminders go to everyone. The first person to act marks it done, and it disappears for the rest.

**Invite someone in 30 seconds:**

<center>

[**Invite a Household Member →**]({{invite_member_link}})

</center>

You can invite family, a roommate, a pet sitter — anyone who helps care for your pets. You control their role (admin, member, or sitter) and what they can see.

Once two people are logging activities, PetForce goes from useful to indispensable.

— The PetForce Team

## Email 3: Tips + Feedback Ask

**Trigger:** Day 7 after signup
**Subject:** "One week in — here's what we've noticed"
**Condition:** Send to all active users (at least 1 activity logged)

---

Hi {{first_name}},

You've been using PetForce for a week. Here's a quick look at what your household has logged:

> **{{activity_count}} activities** logged for **{{pet_count}} pet(s)** by **{{member_count}} household member(s)**

A few tips to get more out of PetForce:

**Use quick-log for routine care.** Tap the + button on your dashboard for one-tap feeding, walking, and medication logs. It pre-fills based on time of day and your pet's species.

**Check the care timeline.** Each pet's profile shows a complete history — helpful when you need to tell the vet exactly when symptoms started or what they last ate.

**Set up feeding schedules.** Go to any pet's profile and choose a species-based template. PetForce will remind your whole household when it's time.

- What's the thing you use most?
