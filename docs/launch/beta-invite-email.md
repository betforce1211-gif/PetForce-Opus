### What you can do right now

- **Create your household** and invite your family, partner, or pet sitter
- **Add your pets** with their species, breed, and any medical notes
- **Log care activities** — feedings, walks, vet visits, medications — in under 3 seconds

### Why we built this

Pet care is a team sport, but most families coordinate it with text messages, sticky notes, and "I thought you fed him." PetForce gives your whole household a shared view of who did what, when — so nothing falls through the cracks.
