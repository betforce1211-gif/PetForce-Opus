# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-03-09

PetForce ("we", "us", "our") operates the PetForce mobile application and website (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (via Clerk authentication)
- Display name
- Profile information you provide

### Pet Data
- Pet names, species, breeds, dates of birth, weight
- Health records (vet visits, vaccinations, medications)
- Feeding schedules and completion logs
- Activity logs (walks, grooming, play sessions)
- Photos you upload

### Household Data
- Household name and settings
- Member roles and relationships
- Expense records
- Notes and journals

### Usage Data
- Feature usage patterns (anonymized)
- Error logs for debugging
- Device type and browser version

## How We Use Your Data

- **Provide the Service** — store and display your pet care data
- **Household sharing** — share data with members you invite
- **Notifications** — send reminders about feedings, medications, and appointments
- **Improvements** — analyze anonymized usage patterns to improve the product

## Data Storage

- Your data is stored in PostgreSQL databases hosted by Supabase (US data centers)
- Authentication is handled by Clerk
- Pet photos are stored in Supabase Storage
- We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal data. We share data only:
- With household members you explicitly invite
- With service providers (Supabase, Clerk) necessary to operate the Service
- When required by law

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Access** your data through the app at any time
- **Export** your data (feature coming soon)
- **Delete** your account and all associated data by contacting us
- **Remove** household members and revoke invitations

## Children's Privacy

PetForce is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at privacy@petforce.app.
