Paw Circle — 10 Variations

Starting from the PF Monogram concept. The circle becomes a cute paw. Each explores a different paw style.

01 — Classic

Classic Round

Perfectly symmetrical circles. Clean, recognizable, reads as "paw" instantly at any size.

02 — Chubby

Chubby Beans

Wider pad, fatter toe beans, slightly rotated outward. Soft, cute, squishy feel.

03 — Cat Paw

Cat Paw

Elongated oval toes angled outward, heart-shaped main pad. More anatomically cat-like.

04 — Dog Paw

Dog Paw

Wider spread toes, chunkier pads, bigger footprint. Sturdy, friendly, dog energy.

05 — Tilted

Tilted Playful

Same clean paw, rotated 15 degrees. Adds movement and personality. Feels like a stamp.

06 — Three Toes

Three Toes

Simplified to three toes instead of four. More abstract, more logo-friendly. Reads clearly at tiny sizes.

07 — Heart Pad

Heart Pad

The main pad is a heart shape. Love + pets. Cute without being childish.

08 — Compact

Compact

Toes packed close to the pad. Forms a tighter, more circular overall silhouette. Great for small spaces.

09 — Splayed

Splayed

Toes spread wide like an excited pounce. Dynamic, energetic — fits the "Force" theme.

10 — Kawaii Blob

Kawaii Blob

All circles, slightly asymmetric sizes. Cute, approachable, bubbly personality. Think app icon energy.
