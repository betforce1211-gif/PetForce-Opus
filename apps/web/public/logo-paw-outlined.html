Outlined Paw — PF / Bolt / House

Line-art paw style. Each explores a different way to integrate the PF initials, a lightning bolt, and/or a house into the pad.

01 — PF in Pad

PF in Pad

Bold "PF" initials sit inside the main pad where the heart would be. Direct, legible, monogram feel.

02 — Bolt in Pad

Bolt in Pad

Lightning bolt replaces the heart. Two-tone — indigo paw, gold bolt. "Force" speaks for itself.

03 — House in Pad

House in Pad

Simple house outline inside the pad. Paw + home = PetForce. Clean line-on-line.

04 — PF Bolt

P + Lightning F

The "P" is a letter, the "F" is a lightning bolt. PF = Pet Force, visually. Two-tone trick.

05 — House-Heart

House-Heart

The heart from the reference gets a roof peak added above it. Love + home fused. Subtle and clever.

06 — Bolt Toe

Bolt Toe

One toe replaced by a lightning bolt. Heart stays in the pad. Keeps the cute reference style, adds "Force" as a surprise.

07 — House + PF + Bolt

House Roof + PF + Bolt

All three elements inside the pad: roof line above, PF below, small bolt accent. The full story in one mark.

08 — House IS the Pad

House IS the Pad

The main pad shape is literally a house (with a peaked roof top). Four toes above. The pad and the house are one.

09 — Bolt Through

Bolt Through

Big diagonal lightning bolt slashes across the whole paw. Dramatic, bold, unmistakable "Force" energy.

10 — Everything Layered

Roof + PF + Bolt Chimney

A-frame roof inside the pad with PF underneath and a bolt chimney. All three elements, layered cleanly.
